Rust for the Skeptical Backend Dev

You don't need to rewrite your service in Rust. But there are three places — a hot path, a CLI, a sidecar — where it quietly wins. I'll show what porting each felt like from a Go/Node shop, what the borrow checker actually costs a team, and when to walk away.
